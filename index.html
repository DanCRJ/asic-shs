SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

ASiC is offering Senior High School which aims at continuously providing high quality education to become global leaders. Our SHS students will be trained by expert faculty, industry practitioners and nationally certified instructors. Our SHS curricula are focused on relevant industry courses to ensure employability, better career options and valued credentials for student’s future opportunities and lifelong learning experiences. Our SHS programs will utilize varied delivery options such as eLearning platform with appropriate supports and quality services that are suitable to meet the individual needs of Grades 11 and 12 students.

ASiC Senior High School courses will be the model for student’s lifelong access opportunities in pursuing higher education and/or further training. This includes a variety of academic options and industry work-experiences and credit transfer agreements.
